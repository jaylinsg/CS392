PS 41 (The Stephanie A. Vierno School)

Elementary School

I attended PS 41 from kindergarten through fifth grade—years that shaped who I am today, even if some details have faded. Many of the friendships I made there still endure. The highlight of my time was serving as school president in fifth grade: to campaign, I performed a rap outlining my vision for the school—and it won me the election! One change I implemented has become a lasting tradition. On rainy days, when recess moved indoors, I transformed the cafeteria into an open-mic stage, inviting students to perform songs, poems, or any talent they had. That experience taught me the power of creativity and leadership values which I carry with me in every new role I take on.
